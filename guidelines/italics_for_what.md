# What are our rules for Pylon about when to use italics?

Do NOT use italics

- Not for "e.g.", "cf."
  - not for the names of Greek letters - so not for sigma, epsilon etc.

Use italics for

- For Latin words and phrases
  - *ed.pr.*
  - *editio princeps*

- For Greek words in Latin characters
  - *ekdosimon*
  - *pronoetes*
